Class 12

Advanced learning, tests, and research

  • Scored 83.8% in Class 12, including 99% in Web Application.
  • Attended AP Computer Science A.
  • Attended AP Calculus AB.
  • Wrote my first research paper.
  • Admitted to CCIR with the CCIR STEM Scholarship.

  • Built a full-wave rectifier from scratch.
  • SAT Tutor on schoolhouse.world, the official SAT tutoring platform supported by College Board and Khan Academy.
  • Selected as a contributor at GirlScript Summer of Code open-source program.
