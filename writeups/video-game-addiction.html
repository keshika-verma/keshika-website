Psychology & Personal Growth

Video Game Addiction

Written during the Covid-19 Pandemic/8th Grade. "Over the past year, I have had to listen to the terms, "Computer Vision Syndrome", "Computer Fatigue", etc, a lot, especially from my parents. If you've been through the same, this write-up is for you".

Does it ever feel like you are thinking only about games? Feeling bad, irritable, or moody when you cannot play? Not wanting to do other things you used to long for? Lying about your screen time? Not being able to concentrate on studies?.......

Here's all that I've heard about video games recently:

It is great to do things you enjoy. But here is a point: can you go too far with video games? And at what point do these become an addiction?

You spend a lot of time on them, indirectly wasting your time and stopping yourself from thinking about your studies or education anymore. You connect yourself to Wi-Fi rather than people, facilitate laziness, and get yourself an indirect licence to be hurtful on losing game lives.

You may lose emotions and may leave no difference between killing your enemy in a game and in real life, as has been proved in some cases. A more ridiculous drawback is that you may gain weight by sitting for hours in the same place. You may not be able to stop your gaming hobby even if you try, and being unsuccessful in life is the last thing anyone would ever wish for.

Well, chill.

This is not to criticize those who play. Video games are not always bad. “Games” is a topic that divides opinion. Some are worried about its impact, while for a few, it is an amazing tool.

If you set play timings and stick to them, can differentiate between study time and playtime, spend more time on outdoor games, play games related only to your age, and have a good relationship with your parents, then gaming is unlikely to be a problem.

In addition, studies prove that if you are a good gamer, you are most likely to be a good decision-maker. Being an expert in this advanced technology is not bad, and gaming has great career opportunities.

Believe it or not, the international video game industry generated a whopping 99.6 billion dollars in 2016, which was more than double the revenue of the international film industry. Isn’t it amazing to know that characters in games are bigger celebrities than our movie stars?

Also, when the first game was invented back in the early 1970s, interestingly, within 15 minutes, every examiner on the floor started wanting to play the game. We could see those adults fighting over a video game, and then they say kids are addicted to it.

But for one who wishes to have a nice and well-dominant personality and be a big-picture thinker, my best recommendation is this: along with the video games you play, you must also adopt a crazily amazing book addiction and give equal concentration to your studies too.

None, not even your parents, can stop you from playing then. They must look no further for ways to improve you. If your video game time is getting out of hand, manage yourself and be cool, as well as a lifelong pro!

- KESHIKA VERMA
