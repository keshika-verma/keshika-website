Science & Curiosity

The Universe + Biology = ?

Biology is the study of life, as we have been told millions of times, and almost every science student knows it well. But what comes to our mind when we hear the word “Universe”?

Black space, a picture of the solar system, the asteroid belt, the moon, the Earth, and so much more. The Universe, in itself, is an enthralling term. It has pushed mankind for ages to research and wonder about it. From Aristotle to almost every second curious person in today’s world, people have wanted to know more about the Universe.

Space research is very interesting to many, while to some others it may seem completely boring because it appears to have no direct bearing on most people’s lives. But actually, it does relate to everyone.

We are life on this Earth. But have you ever wondered how it all began? Science or God? How did life in the universe evolve? And if we talk about careers, what if someone likes both biology and astronomy? What subject should they choose?

Here comes the answer: Astrobiology.

Astrobiology, formerly known as exobiology, is an interdisciplinary scientific field that studies the origins, early evolution, distribution, and future of life in the universe. Astrobiology considers the question of whether extraterrestrial life exists, and if it does, how humans can detect it.

Sounds interesting, right? How cool would it be to study life beyond Earth, stars, planets, galaxies, and everything in between?

Still, the field of astrobiology is very new in India. Not many educational centres offer education in this combination of Universe + Biology. However, the career opportunities in this field are numerous. Even NASA has separate programs related to it.

So, Universe + Biology = a very interesting field known as Astrobiology.
