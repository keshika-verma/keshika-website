Digital Diplomacy: A Global Game Changer

Ladies and gentlemen,

Imagine a world where a single message, sent from the remote reaches of the Arctic, can echo almost instantly in the halls of power across continents. That’s the power of digital diplomacy.

Hi everyone! I’m Keshika Verma, and I am here to affirm that digital diplomacy is indeed a game changer.

Prime Minister Narendra Modi has called on his ambassadors to “remain ahead of the curve on digital diplomacy.” That is the reason no one can forget India’s powerful Vaccine Maitri Initiative, which reached countries across the globe.

In a recent war crisis, Ukraine used social media and delivered virtual addresses to the United Nations to garner worldwide support, all digitally.

Some might worry that digital diplomacy is insecure. However, digital security has become stronger than ever, enabling faster and more inclusive global conversations.

Digitalization allows nations to communicate effectively, which is crucial for tackling global issues like climate change. Even right now, we are all connected digitally and conducting highly intellectual discussions. That itself speaks volumes.

As I conclude, I say that heroes like Maya Angelou, Martin Luther King Jr., and thousands of others must be proud. Thanks to digital diplomacy, every nation can now tell its own story to the world.

Thanks for giving me your ears, digitally.

- KESHIKA VERMA
